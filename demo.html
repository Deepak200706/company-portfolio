Welcome to M. A. Rubber Products

M. A. Rubber Products is a Chennai-based micro-enterprise specializing in the manufacturing of a wide variety of precision rubber components. Established with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, the company has steadily built a reputation for delivering durable, reliable, and customizable rubber products for diverse industrial applications.

With a focus on O-rings, gaskets, rubber contraceptives, and custom rubber molding, we cater to both bulk and OEM orders. Our manufacturing unit is equipped to handle various client-specific requirements, ensuring timely production and compliance with industrial standards.

We are officially recognized as a micro-enterprise under the MSME category and are certified with:
ISO: 24EQMD67
GST: 33EVPPP808F1Z7

The firm is managed by Madhan M (ITI) and Priyadharshini R (M.A.), who bring hands-on expertise and a client-focused approach to operations. We proudly serve customers across Tamil Nadu and other regions with integrity and consistency.
